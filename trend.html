The "Lost and Found" Chicago, also known as the 2022 "Lost and Found", is a sneaker that pays homage to the original 1985 Air Jordan 1. The shoe's design is inspired by what an original 1985 Air Jordan 1 would look like if found decades later in a dusty stock room.
